About Us

Faculty of Science and Technology at MRU is a center of academic excellence dedicated to achieving scientific knowledge, innovation and technological development.The faculty provides quality education that blends theory with practical application to prepare graduates who are competitive both locally and globally.

We are home to a wide range of academic programs in Computer Science, Information Technology, Civil Engineering and related fields, all designed to equip students with the skills and competences needed to address real-world challenges.
